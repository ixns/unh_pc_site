Programming Club

Who are we?

We are Computer Science, Applied Math, Electrical Engineering, etc.. students
who solve algorithm problems to become better programmers.

Do I need to be good at programming?

No, you come to our club to get good at programming.

How to join?

To join the club, you must first sign up for Kattis.
After you've done that, join our Discord and follow the instructions in #do-this.

When do we meet?

Every Thursday from 7:00pm to 8:30pm in Kingsbury N204.

Any other questions?
